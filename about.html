About Rawitphoom Kiathitinan

I am Rawitphoom, a dedicated student exploring the intersection of design and technology. My focus lies in creating user-centered designs and functional web experiences. With skills in UI/UX design, coding, and prototyping, I enjoy solving problems through thoughtful design.

I began my journey with a curiosity for how design and technology could work together. Over time, I’ve developed hands-on experience using tools like Figma, Protopie, Adobe Photoshop, Framer, and HTML/CSS/JS. Each project has helped me grow as a designer and developer.

From building interactive apps to crafting responsive websites, I enjoy every step of the process—from understanding user needs to bringing ideas to life. I value collaboration, clear communication, and attention to detail in every project I take on.
