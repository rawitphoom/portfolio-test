Whimsy Bites

IAT339 - Web Design and Development

Duration

March 2025 - April 2025 (1 Months)

Project Links

Github Website: https://rawitphoom.github.io/Whimsy-test/home.html

Concept & Overview

Whimsy Bites is a playful dessert brand website designed to capture a sense of fun, sweetness, and charm through bold pink tones, soft typography, and friendly visuals. The website focuses on creating an inviting first impression while clearly presenting the brand story, menu highlights, and ordering information. The goal was to design a visually appealing and easy-to-navigate experience that reflects the joyful personality of the brand and makes users feel excited about desserts.

Process & Technical Skills

The website was designed in Figma and developed using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a strong focus on front-end visuals and responsive behavior. A pink-forward color palette was paired with soft shadows, rounded components, and subtle animations to reinforce the whimsical brand identity. Reusable components such as dessert cards, buttons, and section layouts were created to keep the design consistent across the site while allowing flexibility for future updates.

Challenges

One challenge was maintaining visual playfulness without sacrificing clarity. With strong colors and decorative elements, careful spacing and hierarchy were needed to ensure content remained easy to scan. Another challenge involved responsive design, especially scaling dessert images and typography across different screen sizes while keeping the layout balanced and polished.

Outcome & Learning

The final Whimsy Bites website delivers a cohesive and joyful brand experience that feels friendly, modern, and inviting. The project strengthened my skills in visual storytelling, color-driven UI design, and responsive front-end development. The result is a website that clearly communicates brand personality while remaining functional and easy to use.
